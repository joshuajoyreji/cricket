Rules

You can either choose to bat or ball. There are buttons provided for numbers from 1 to 6.
To BAT, select any number(from 1 to 6) to add to your score. Once computer selects same number as yours, you will be out.
To BALL, select any number(from 1 to 6). Once computer selects same number as yours, you will be out. The team getting the highest runs wins the match. ALL THE BEST!
